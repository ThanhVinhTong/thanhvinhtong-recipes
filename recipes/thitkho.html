Vietnamese Braised Pork with Eggs (Thit kho)

Tempting to savour a magnificent Pork stew-like dish? U have come to the right place.

Here are the ingredients you need to prepare this dish:

Ingredients Amount
  • pork belly
  • quail eggs (or chicken/duck eggs)
  • coconut water
  • shallot, garlic clove, chili, green onion
  • Seasonings: Fish sauce, sugar, salt, pepper, MSG, cooking oil
  • 500g
  • 10 eggs
  • 400ml
  • 1 each
  • up to u

Steps:

Prepare ingredients:

  • Clean and cut pork into square chunks (about 3x3 cm).
  • Boil eggs for 15 minutes, then peel.
  • Chop shallots, garlic, and chili. Slice green onions.

Marinate the pork:

  • Mix pork with fish sauce, salt, sugar, pepper, and oil.
  • Let it sit for at least 1 hour.

Make caramel sauce:

  • Heat sugar in a pot until it turns dark brown, then add water.
  • Stir to prevent burning and bitterness.

Braise the pork:

  • Sauté garlic and shallots, then add the marinated pork.
  • Cook until browned, then add chili, coconut water, and caramel sauce.
  • Add peeled boiled eggs and enough water to cover.
  • Simmer for about 30 minutes.

Serve:

  • Garnish with green onions and serve hot with rice, bánh chưng, or bánh tét.

Beef with bordelaise sauce (Bo sot vang)

Tempting to savour a delicious Beef stew-like dish? U have come to the right place.

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients you need to prepare this dish:

Ingredients Amount

  • Red wine

  • Potato

  • Unsalted butter

  • Beef stew seasoning packet

Instructions

Here are the steps to prepare this dish:

  1. Prepare the vegetables:
    • Tomatoes: Wash, cut into wedges, remove seeds, blend or mash.
    • Carrots & Potatoes: Peel, cut into bite-sized chunks.
    • Onions, Ginger, Garlic: Dice onions, slice ginger, mince garlic.
    • Sauté Onions & Tomato Sauce: Cook onions in butter, then add tomato sauce and simmer for 3 minutes.
  2. Prepare the beef:
    • Cut beef into cube-sized pieces.
    • Blanch in hot water with ginger for 1 minute to remove odors.
    • Alternative odor removal: Rub with roasted ginger or rinse with vinegar/wine.
  3. Marinate the beef:
    • Mix beef with 1 pack of stew seasoning and 10ml red wine.
    • Let sit for 30 minutes.
  4. Saute the beef:
    • Melt butter, sauté garlic, then stir-fry beef for 7 minutes.
    • Add enough water to cover the meat.
  5. Simmer the stew:
    • Add tomato sauce and simmer for 60 minutes.
    • Add carrots, cook for 15 minutes, then add potatoes and cook until tender.
    • Thicken with tapioca starch mixture, then add ketchup and remaining wine.
    • Adjust seasoning and simmer briefly.
  6. Enjoy!
    • The stew has a rich, slightly thick sauce with tender beef and flavorful vegetables.
    • Serve with bread, rice noodles, or hot rice.
    • Store leftovers in the fridge for 1-2 days.
